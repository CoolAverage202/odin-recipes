World's finest Pizza!

The concept of the simple, yet delicious Pepperoni Pizza:

Have you ever wanted to create a pizza of your own. Well with this simple recipe it is possible.

Please try it and let us know how it went!

Ingredients:

  • 16 ounces pizza dough, store-bought or homemade(this makes 2 pizzas)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce (see below)
  • 18 to 20 sliced pepperoni
  • 12 ounces mozzerella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano, optional
  • Flour for rolling and shaping dough

Pizza Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce(no salt)
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon granulated onion
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven to 500 degrees Fahrenheit.( if on pizza stone Preheat for 20 mins)
  2. Make Sauce.
  3. Roll out the dough: Rest for 5 mins (large roll pizza to 14-inch diameter circle)
  4. Add the toppings.
  5. Cook the pizza
  6. Slice and serve.
